●  A Short Story

A journey I began 33 years ago — the last 13 years dedicated to Real Estate.

I started my active business life in Istanbul in 1994. Ever since, every venture I undertook I saw through to completion — handed the keys to the owner personally, and stood behind my work. That is the rule of my latest venture, my Real Estate Marketing Office, and it is mine.

In 2008 I founded Avla Doğa Mimarları. Since that day I have built roads, fields, gardens; I led the infrastructure works of Istanbul's largest projects. Since 2022 I have been actively practicing Real Estate Investment Advisory.

Like every job I do, I make expertise the foundation. The projects I recommend to you in Greece are the result of deep research and informed scrutiny — current and future value, location, and developer character all examined in depth.

Today I travel between Istanbul and Athens at least twice a month. The vast majority of my clients bought their Athens apartment without seeing it first. The reason is one and only one: trust.

I listen to my clients carefully; together we determine their character, what matters to them, and the items on their list of needs. Face-to-face meetings, on-site inspection, presentation and a personalized shortlist — they rescue clients from the confusion they fall into during decision-making.

One thing must not be forgotten: before choosing a property, in the journey of acquiring that property, you must choose your advisor — and establishing mutual loyalty and trust is essential. Because this is your choice.
